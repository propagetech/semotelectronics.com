GRIDSMART ® - 3D OMNIDIRECTIONAL TRACKING / ADVANCED VIDEO-ANALYTICS

This pioneering intersection traffic detection product is making waves in the world ITS market, manufactured and supported by GRIDSMART Technologies, Inc., a Knoxville, Tennessee, USA based company. The GRIDSMART System is the world’s most trusted and only field-tested, single camera system that gathers and interprets important traffic data , empowers traffic engineers to adjust signal timing and traffic flow strategies, and enables real-time monitoring and visual assessment. The GRIDSMART system includes the iconic Bell Camera, GS2 Processor, and Client software.

GRIDSMART pioneered horizon to horizon vision tracking to actuate intersections with the iconic Bell Camera, a single fisheye camera providing what others can’t: a full intersection view, including the center where cars, trucks, motorcycles and scooters, bicycles, and pedestrians actually cross paths. The Bell Camera is mounted ten meters high and the horizon to horizon approach offers highly accurate turn counts, unmatched situational awareness, views and functionality from the center of the intersection, and unobstructed incident management views.

The camera’s virtual pan-tilt-zoom works with a simple mouse click, enabling users to set up to four different views and adjust those anytime they wish. The aesthetically pleasing, environmentally conducive bell shape of the camera isn’t just for looks. The rugged 1⁄4-inch-thick aluminum housing protects the optics from the harshest environments and prevents weather related blind spots and sun glare. It is easy to install and maintain, thereby reducing the number and length of lane closures associated with intersection management, while also improving worker safety, and reducing costs.

The Bell Camera is powered from a single-wire, power over ethernet (POE) connection by the GS2 Processor. GS2, the intelligence of the GRIDSMART System, runs the GRIDSMART Engine, a suite of vision-tracking algorithms that builds a 3-dimensional model of cars, trucks, pedestrians, and other objects approaching the intersection. The object trajectories are tracked through user-defined zones at the intersection, following them until vehicles exit, delivering unmatched accuracy. GS2 can support two Bell Cameras or one Bell and four traditional cameras.

The Client empowers you to set up detection and counting zones, view intersections and highways, and even generate performance reports . GRIDSMART’s data modules upload valuable performance data daily to the GRIDSMART Cloud. The open API retrieves data and images from GRIDSMART Processors or automates the retrieval of count or real-time data.

GRIDSMART interfaces with any standard intersection traffic controller to function as a traffic responsive traffic controller.

GRIDSMART has counted and classified more than 8 billion vehicles around the world with unmatched accuracy, delivering safe and efficient solutions in intersections spanning across 1,200 cities, 49 states, and 29 countries. GRIDSMART’s unmatched accuracy is perfected through 54 million hours of real-world video processed across all environmental conditions.

Special Features:
  • No need to aim or focus the Bell Camera
  • Single camera does the work of four traditional cameras
  • Suitable for Indian traffic with mixed traffic and no lane discipline
  • Free cloud connectivity for all units
  • Multiple communication ports & interfaces for traffic controller
  • Simple and quick to install, no cumbersome wiring for multiple cameras
  • 24X7 – data collection
